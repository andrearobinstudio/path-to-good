Dear Ordinary — the series map

A 56-week path through queering contemplative spirituality, arranged as eight constellations of stars. Open any week to see the star it navigates by and the territory it explores.

Each week draws one star into three movements — grounding, ethics, and emergence — and closes with an invitation into the studio.

Eight constellations of equal standing · thirty-five stars · no fixed sequence.
